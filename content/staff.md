## Research, engineering, and operations

Staff support research programs, engineering work, operations, partnerships, and the public community around EleutherAI.

### Research staff

Researchers lead and contribute to work across language modeling, interpretability, alignment, evaluation, datasets, and other open research programs.

### Operations and institutional staff

Operations staff support grants, partnerships, programs, communications, finance, and the day-to-day institutional work that lets research continue.

### Regular collaborators and volunteers

EleutherAI's open-collaboration model includes regular volunteers and external collaborators who contribute to research, code, data, and community work.
