EleutherAI's research is organized around making frontier-scale AI research possible outside a small number of closed labs. We build and maintain open-source infrastructure for training, evaluating, and studying models, with an emphasis on tools, datasets, and releases that let independent researchers reproduce results, audit behavior, and ask new scientific questions.

We are especially focused on evaluations, interpretability over time, and open-source safety. That means studying how models change during training, how capabilities and failures can be measured clearly, how internal mechanisms emerge and shift, and what safety practices are needed when powerful systems can be inspected, modified, and redeployed by the broader research community.
