About

A nonprofit AI research lab focused on open, inspectable foundation-model research.

EleutherAI works on interpretability, alignment, language models, and the infrastructure needed for independent researchers to study increasingly capable AI systems.

Mission

Why EleutherAI exists

Large-scale AI research is expensive and technically demanding. EleutherAI's role is to make parts of the scientific record public: artifacts, methods, analyses, and collaboration routes that are not restricted to a few closed labs.

Access

Independent study should remain possible

Researchers need access to models, checkpoints, datasets, and evaluations in order to understand how foundation models work and fail.

Mission
Safety

Interpretability and alignment need open evidence

Understanding model internals, behavior, and risks requires artifacts that can be inspected and compared by the broader research community.
