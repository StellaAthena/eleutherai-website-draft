Open research infrastructure for understanding AI.

Research

We investigate how models learn, generalize, fail, and change across training, data, and deployment contexts.

  • Training dynamics
  • Circuits and interpretability
  • Open-weight safety

Public artifacts

We release model suites, datasets, benchmarks, and tools for reproducible work.

  • Models and checkpoints
  • Evaluation systems
  • Dataset curation

Institution

A nonprofit lab for open, inspectable AI research.

EleutherAI grew from an open research community into a nonprofit institute focused on interpretability, alignment, and open foundation-model research. The lab's work combines staff research, volunteer collaboration, external partnerships, and public releases that make independent study of AI systems possible.
