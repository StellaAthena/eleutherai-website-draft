Community

Open research, organized in public

EleutherAI is far more than just its staff: it’s a global community of AI researchers, engineers, and enthusiasts interested in participating in and discussing cutting-edge AI research.

As part of our mission to bring greater accessibility to AI research, we welcome people from all backgrounds to collaborate with us on research.

Some community researchers join projects planned and lead by EleutherAI staff, while others receive mentorship, guidance, and computing resources to turn their own ideas into reality.

EleutherAI is organized in a public discord server, and anyone in the world can stop by and read about our on-going work, join meetings, and get involved. Our practice of doing science in the open brings unprecedented transparency and openness to our work.

No PhD? No problem! EleutherAI was founded by software engineers and AI hobbyists, and to this day the majority of people who participate in our research do not have a PhD. Strong engineering skills, a passion for open source, and familiarity with machine learning are more important attributes for potential collaborators.

In addition to being an open research institute, EleutherAI is a locus for discussion of cutting edge research and engineering that powers large scale artificial intelligence. Leading researchers from academia and industry alike come together to discuss the latest papers and ideas in our discussion channels.
